# Amazon Monitron

Amazon Monitron is an end-to-end predictive maintenance system that uses machine learning to detect abnormal conditions in industrial equipment.

## Operations & Industrial AI

### End-to-End Predictive Maintenance
Monitron is unique because it provides both the physical hardware and the ML-powered software required to monitor equipment health and predict failures before they happen, reducing unplanned downtime.

### System Components
- **Monitron Sensors**: Physical IoT sensors that you attach to industrial machinery (like motors, gearboxes, or pumps) to capture vibration and temperature data.
- **Monitron Gateways**: Devices that aggregate data from the sensors and securely transmit it to the AWS cloud over Wi-Fi or Ethernet.
- **ML Analysis Software**: The backend service automatically analyzes the telemetry data using machine learning models to establish baselines and detect anomalies indicative of wear or impending failure.
- **Mobile App**: A companion app for technicians to receive alerts about abnormal equipment conditions, allowing them to take preventive action.
