# Amazon Transcribe

Amazon Transcribe is an Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) service that makes it easy for developers to add speech-to-text capability to their applications.

## Audio & Speech Orchestration

### Core Capabilities
Transcribe converts audio files or live audio streams into highly accurate, time-stamped text transcripts.

- **Batch & Real-Time**: Can process pre-recorded audio files stored in S3 or process live audio streams via WebSockets or HTTP/2.
- **Speaker Diarization**: Capable of recognizing when the speaker changes in a multi-speaker audio file, labeling the transcript to identify who said what (e.g., "Speaker 1" vs "Speaker 2").
- **Custom Vocabulary**: Allows you to add specific words, acronyms, or industry-specific terminology to the service's base vocabulary to improve transcription accuracy.
- **Medical & Call Analytics**: Transcribe offers specialized variants (Transcribe Medical and Transcribe Call Analytics) optimized for clinical documentation and customer service call centers, respectively.
