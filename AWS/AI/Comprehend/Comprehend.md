# Amazon Comprehend

Amazon Comprehend is a Natural Language Processing (NLP) service that uses machine learning to find insights and relationships in text.

## Natural Language Processing (NLP) Capabilities

Comprehend can analyze text blocks (like customer reviews, emails, or support tickets) to extract valuable metadata without requiring you to train any ML models yourself.

### Key Extractions
- **Key Phrases**: Extracts the main talking points or noun phrases from a document (e.g., "fast shipping", "broken screen").
- **Entities**: Identifies named entities such as people, places, organizations, dates, and commercial items.
- **Syntax**: Tokenizes text and identifies the part of speech (noun, verb, adjective) for each word.
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Determines the emotional tone of the text, categorizing it as Positive, Negative, Neutral, or Mixed.

*Comprehend is heavily used for unstructured text analytics, helping businesses route customer support tickets, monitor brand sentiment on social media, or categorize vast document libraries.*
