# Amazon Personalize

Amazon Personalize is a fully managed machine learning service that enables developers to build real-time, highly customized recommendation engines.

## Real-Time Recommendation Engine

### Core Architecture
Personalize is powered by the exact same machine learning technology utilized by Amazon.com for their retail product recommendations. It removes the need for deep ML expertise by automating the complex steps of building, training, and deploying recommendation models.

### Key Features
- **Customized for your data**: You provide data about your users (demographics), your items (catalog), and the interactions between them (clicks, purchases, views). Personalize builds a unique model based purely on your data.
- **Real-Time Updates**: The models can adapt to user intent in real-time. If a user suddenly starts clicking on "running shoes", the recommendations will instantly shift to reflect that new interest.
- **Specific Use Cases**: Offers optimized recipes for specific business goals, such as "Users who viewed X also viewed", "Recommended for you", or personalized search ranking.
