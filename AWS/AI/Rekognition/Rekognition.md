# Amazon Rekognition

Amazon Rekognition is a fully managed computer vision service that makes it easy to add powerful visual analysis to your applications.

## Core Capabilities

### Image and Video Analysis
Automates the extraction of information from images and videos. It can identify objects (cars, furniture), scenes (city, beach), and activities (playing sports, delivering a package), providing highly accurate bounding boxes and confidence scores.

### Face Liveness & Comparison
- **Face Liveness**: A feature to verify that a user going through a biometric identity verification process is a real, live person (preventing spoofing attacks using printed photos or screens).
- **Face Comparison & Search**: Compares faces across images to determine if they are the same person, or searches a private repository of face images to identify individuals.

### Text in Image
Uses Optical Character Recognition (OCR) optimized for images in the wild. It can extract text like street signs, license plates, superimposed captions on news broadcasts, or visual copy on packaging.

### Content Moderation
Provides automated flagging of unsafe, explicit, or inappropriate visual material (such as nudity, violence, or visually disturbing content). It returns a hierarchical list of labels with confidence scores, allowing you to build business rules to automatically blur, block, or route content for manual review.
