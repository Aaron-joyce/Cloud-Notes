# Amazon Fraud Detector

Amazon Fraud Detector is a fully managed risk-analysis service built to flag potentially fraudulent and malicious online activity.

## Analytics & Predictive Modeling for Security

### Automated Risk Analysis
Fraud Detector uses machine learning models and rules to evaluate online activities in real-time, assigning a risk score that helps businesses decide whether to allow, review, or block a transaction.

### Key Capabilities
- **Tailored ML Models**: The service uses your historical data (both legitimate and fraudulent events) to train a custom machine learning model tailored to your specific business patterns.
- **Common Use Cases**:
  - **New Account Fraud**: Flagging the creation of fake accounts.
  - **Account Takeover (ATO)**: Detecting unusual login behaviors indicating compromised credentials.
  - **Online Payment Fraud**: Identifying suspicious online purchases or checkout attempts.
- **Rules Engine**: Allows you to combine the ML risk score with explicit business rules (e.g., "If Risk Score > 900 AND IP is from a blocklist -> REJECT").
