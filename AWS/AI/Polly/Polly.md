# Amazon Polly

Amazon Polly is a service that turns text into lifelike speech, allowing you to create applications that talk and build entirely new categories of speech-enabled products.

## Text-to-Speech (TTS) Engine

### Standard and Neural TTS
Polly offers dozens of lifelike voices across a broad set of languages.
- **Standard TTS**: Uses concatenative synthesis to deliver fast, highly intelligible speech.
- **Neural TTS (NTTS)**: Uses advanced deep learning technologies to synthesize speech that sounds incredibly natural and human-like, capturing nuances in intonation and rhythm.

### Advanced Features
- **Speech Synthesis Markup Language (SSML)**: Supports SSML tags, allowing you to heavily customize the pronunciation, volume, pitch, speed, and emphasis of the generated speech.
- **Newscaster & Conversational Styles**: Certain NTTS voices support specific speaking styles tailored for reading news articles or engaging in casual two-way dialogue.
- **Speech Marks**: Allows developers to synchronize the generated audio with visual elements (like lip-syncing an avatar or highlighting text as it is read) by returning a metadata stream alongside the audio.
