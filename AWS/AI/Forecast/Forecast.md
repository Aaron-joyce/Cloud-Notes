# Amazon Forecast

Amazon Forecast is a fully managed service that uses machine learning to deliver highly accurate time-series forecasts.

## Time-Series Forecasting

### Predictive Modeling
Forecast is designed to analyze historical time-series data points and predict future variations. It excels at finding complex patterns that traditional statistical forecasting methods (like ARIMA) often miss.

### Key Capabilities
- **Incorporating Related Data**: Unlike simple forecasting that only looks at past sales to predict future sales, Forecast allows you to add related variables that impact demand—such as weather, holiday schedules, price changes, or store locations.
- **Automated AutoML**: You don't need to choose the ML algorithm; Forecast automatically inspects your data and tests multiple algorithms (including deep learning algorithms like DeepAR+) to find the most accurate model for your specific dataset.
- **Use Cases**: Commonly used for retail inventory planning, supply chain optimization, workforce scheduling, and financial capacity planning.
